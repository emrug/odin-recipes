Caprese salad

A caprese salad is a summer classic for a reason. You can only make it if your tomatoes are ripe and juicy, otherwise it will be a sad and disapointing dish. Good quality olive oil, balsamic and mozzerella are also important for making sure the dish does not taste bland.

Ingredients

  • A small bunch of basil
  • 4 balls of mozzerella
  • 20 medium sized ripe tomatoes
  • 8 tbsp extra vigin olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar

Steps

  1. Slice 5 tomatoes into roughly half cm slices, and put the slices onto a dinner plate, then repeat three times. Add a sprinkle of salt over the tomatoes and drizzle over the olive oil, then leave for 10 minutes.
  2. Tear apart a ball of mozzerella into small chunks, then add this to one of the tomato plates. Repeat three times.
  3. Add around 5-10 basil leaves to each plate depending on how big your bunch is, then add a small drizzle of balsamic vinegar. Enjoy!
